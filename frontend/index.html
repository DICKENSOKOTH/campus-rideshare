Simple Process

From Registration to Ride in Minutes

1

Register with Campus Email

Sign up with your university email for instant verification. Drivers upload their license for extra trust.

2

Find or Post a Ride

Search by destination and date, or just tell our AI where you are headed. Drivers post available seats with pricing.

3

Book & Coordinate

Request a seat, get confirmation from your driver, and use in-app chat to arrange pickup details.

4

Ride & Rate

Enjoy your trip! After arrival, rate your experience to help build a trusted community for everyone.
